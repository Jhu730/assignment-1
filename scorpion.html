Deathstalker

The deathstalker scorpion is a venomous species of scorpion that is native to the deserts of North Africa and the Middle East. It is one of the most venomous scorpions in the world and its sting can cause a range of symptoms including intense pain, muscle twitching, nausea, and in severe cases, death. The deathstalker is a yellow-brown color, with a heavy exoskeleton and a distinctive pair of pincers used for capturing its prey.
