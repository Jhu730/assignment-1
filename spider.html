Sydney funnel-web spider

The Sydney funnel-web spider is the most dangerous spider to humans in the world. Native to Australia, this poisonous spider is found in moist habitats such as under logs or in gardens. The funnel-web spider has a distinctive appearance, with a shiny, black body and thick, spiny legs.The Sydney funnel-web has a venom potency of 0.2 mg/kg as a lethal dose for humans.
